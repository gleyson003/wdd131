In our community there are many people who are in situations of hunger and poverty. Because of this, we decided to carry out a social project to collect food and clothes for these people through a volleyball championship.

The championship will have teams formed randomly by registered participants. A total of 6 teams must be formed and will compete against each other. The winning team will be awarded tickets to attend the municipal theater's musical concert.
