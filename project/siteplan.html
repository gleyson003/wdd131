Overview

Solidarity Volleyball

The website has a name that represents its proposal, which is to help people donate clothes and food through a volleyball championship.

Site Purpose

The website proposal is to have 3 pages where its audience can know the championship rules, what to do to participate and whom will be helped with the donations received. So, its will have a form to subscribe and informations about champioship.
